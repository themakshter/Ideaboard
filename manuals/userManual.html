Introduction

Ideas are for keeping, if you let them slip your mind they are gone forever. But they can also be suffocating, having all of them together makes it hard to focus. IdeaBoard allows you to sort and categorise all your ideas into manageable chunks and access them from anywhere.

Notes

All notes support rich text editing including

  • Headers (h1 and h2)
  • Bullet Points
  • Numbered Lists
  • Text styles(bold, italic and underline)
  • HyperLinks

To access any of these option simply highlight the text and click the relevant button to apply the style
